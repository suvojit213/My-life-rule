Mere Niyam

Nimnlikhit nirdesh mere vyaktigat jeevan ke liye hain, jo main roj padhta hoon. Yeh mujhe jeevan mein santulan aur dhyaan banaye rakhne mein madad karte hain.

Mujhe yeh zaroori niyam apne jeevan mein apnane hain, chahe kuch bhi ho. Inka palan karne se mujhe achha mehsoos hoga aur main apni productivity aur mental well-being ko behtar bana sakta hoon.

  1. Jo aapki nahi sunte, unpe apna samay barbaad mat karo.
  2. Hamesha dhyaan se suno, phir javaab do.
  3. Apne kaam par dhyaan do aur dusron ke baare mein zyada mat socho.
  4. Santulan banaye rakho; zyada frank ya hansne se bacho.
  5. Jo aapki kadar nahi karta, waha dobara mat jao.
  6. Agar koi aapke liye bura bol raha ho, tab bhi muskurate raho aur sahi tareeke se jawaab do. Lekin agar baat parivaar ki ho, to chup mat raho.
  7. Gusse ko control rakho; gusse ko aane hi mat do, yeh dimaag ke liye sahi nahi hai.
  8. Kisi se jalna nahi; sabka saath do aur unki safalta mein khushi manao.
